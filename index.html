Hossein Ebrahimi-Kondori

An experienced AI Engineer and Computer Vision Consultant with expertise in Large Language Models (LLMs), computer vision, and machine learning, focusing on developing and deploying cutting-edge solutions.

I hold a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of British Columbia (UBC) and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology. My research and applied interests include LLMs, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Reinforcement Learning, Distributed Training, Data Pipelines, and Cloud Computing (Azure, AWS, DataBricks).
